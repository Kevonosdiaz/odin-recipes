Description

Classic pancake recipe from Allrecipes.

Image and recipe comes from this Allrecipes recipe..

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups AP flour
  • 3 1/2 Tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp white sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1 1/4 Cups milk
  • 3 Tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Sift dry ingredients in a large bowl, and make a well in the center to add the liquids, mixing until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over med-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approx. 1/4 cup per pancake.
  3. Cook until bubbles form and edges are dry, roughly 2-3 mins.
  4. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  5. Repeat with remaining batter and enjoy!
