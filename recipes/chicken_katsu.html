Chicken Katsu

Back to Homepage

Description

A crispy Japanese fried chicken cutlet coated with Panko.

Image and recipe comes from this Allrecipes recipe..

Ingredients

  • 4 Skinless, boneless chicken breast halved, pounded to 1/2 inch thickness
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 Tbsp ap flour
  • 1 Egg, beaten
  • 1 Cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1 Cup oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Place flour, beaten egg, and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes.
  3. Coat chicken breasts in flour, shaking off excess.
  4. Dip coated chicken breast into egg, and press into panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place chicken into hot oil, frying until golden brown, roughly 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Transfer pieces onto a paper towel lined plate to drain oil.
  7. Enjoy the chicken katsu!
